Our research is inspired by the natural history of our study organisms. We often study rattlesnakes as a model for investigating our research questions, along with interests in other systems like barn swallows, mammalian and squamate rattlesnake prey, and others. I'm currently recruiting two PhD students to join the lab in Fall 2024, with opportunities to pursue diverse thesis topics with field, molecular, and computational components.
